What is a clone? Botanically speaking, a clone is a cutting taken from a mature cannabis plant, producing a genetically identical copy of the parent plant. Clones allow you to skip the seedling phase and ensure the characteristics of the mother plant are passed down, guaranteeing consistency in your harvest.
